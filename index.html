About me

Hello, I am Steve Finn. I am currently a sophomore Computer Science and Software Engineering student at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology. I was first introduced to computer science and coding as a sophomore in High School during a Principles of Engineering class. Since then, I have extremely enjoyed learning more coding skills and the many opportunities that are available to accomplish with computer science. I am a part of Residence Life here at Rose-Hulman as a Sophomore Advisor. This position allows me to meet and mentor new freshmen and become their friend as they work through their freshman year.

Outside of school, my main hobbies include golf, reading, and hanging out with friends. I have been playing golf since I was in seventh grade, although I haven't been able to as much since starting college. I also really enjoy watching basically any sport, especially the Colts and PGA Tour. I am also a member of the Pi Kappa Alpha fraternity here at Rose. Through Pike, I have been able to meet many awesome people who have helped me with advice or just cheering me up whenever I am struggling with something.
